# Asset-Brief · Flow-State / Eye-Candy Pack

**Stand:** 2026-05-05 · **Pipeline:** GPT Image 2.0 (Stills) + Seedance 2.0 (Loops)
**Ziel:** Erste Welle — nur Atmosphäre, kein Demo-Storytelling. Welcome-Page,
RolePortal-Tiles, Hero-Backgrounds. Demo-Loops kommen später.

## Globale Regeln (für jeden Prompt)

**Look-Lock — gilt für alle Bilder & Videos:**

> Cinematic editorial photography, German healthcare context, natural daylight
> through tall windows, no clinical sterility. Color palette: warm bone, soft
> stone, muted sage, dusty rose, oxidized brass, raw linen. Skin tones
> believable, no plastic glow, no Instagram filter. Composition with negative
> space, rule-of-thirds. Faces calm, present, not performative — caregiving
> as quiet competence, not heroism. No logos, no medical brand names, no text,
> no captions. Soft 35 mm depth of field, ƒ/2.0–ƒ/2.8.

**Anti-Pattern:** Avoid stock-photo smile, avoid harsh fluorescent light, avoid
blue/green hospital walls, avoid "diversity collage" framing, avoid medical
drama, avoid AI-typical extra fingers and waxy skin.

**Format-Defaults:**
- Hero-Stills: 2400×1350 (16:9), PNG-export
- Portrait-Tiles: 1200×1600 (3:4), PNG-export
- Loops: 1920×1080, 24 fps, 6–8 s, MP4 H.264, kein Ton

## Hinweis für die Pipeline

GPT Image 2.0: bei jedem Prompt explizit `no text, no captions, no logos`
mitgeben — das Modell tendiert sonst zu eingebrannten Wörtern.

Seedance 2.0: bei Loop-Assets explizit `loopable, last frame matches first
frame` ergänzen — sonst gibt es einen sichtbaren Cut-Sprung.
